Elevador - take your projects and ideas to the next level

An open space for workshops, events and exhibitions



Elevador is a place to learn and discover: an open space that offers various activities in the areas of technology, art, literature, creative writing, music etc. It is a place that can be used for workshops and teaching. From time to time, Elevador will also be hosting small concerts and exhibitions.

In addition, Elevador wants to offer an opportunity to people who want to start/conduct group activities such as Meetups, but cannot find or afford a suitable space.

Elevador aims to be a safe and welcoming place for all. Read the Elevador Code of Conduct for more detailed information.
